AI Engineer - Generative AI Platform
Arcane Systems | Austin, TX (Hybrid) | Full-Time

About the Role:
We are looking for an AI Engineer to build and scale our generative AI platform. You will design LLM-powered features, implement retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines, and create AI-driven experiences that delight our users. This is a high-impact role at the intersection of applied AI research and product engineering.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement LLM-based applications using models from OpenAI, Anthropic, and open-source providers
- Build and optimize RAG pipelines integrating vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, or ChromaDB) with large language models
- Develop robust prompt engineering strategies and evaluation frameworks to ensure output quality and reliability
- Fine-tune and adapt foundation models for domain-specific use cases
- Integrate AI capabilities into production applications through well-designed APIs and SDKs
- Implement guardrails, content filtering, and safety mechanisms for AI-generated outputs
- Benchmark model performance across latency, cost, and quality dimensions

Requirements:
- 3+ years of software engineering experience with at least 1 year focused on LLM applications
- Strong Python skills and experience with AI/ML frameworks (LangChain, LlamaIndex, Hugging Face)
- Hands-on experience with vector databases and embedding models
- Understanding of transformer architectures, attention mechanisms, and modern NLP techniques
- Experience with prompt engineering, few-shot learning, and chain-of-thought techniques
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and API development (FastAPI, Flask)
- Knowledge of fine-tuning approaches (LoRA, QLoRA, RLHF)

Nice to Have:
- Experience with multi-modal AI (vision + language models)
- Familiarity with AI agent frameworks and tool-use patterns
- Background in information retrieval or search systems
